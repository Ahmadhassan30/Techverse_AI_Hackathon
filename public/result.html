Theme: AI-Governance for Local Impact

Overview

The Techverse AI Hackathon 2025 brought together innovative teams to address local governance challenges using AI. Held under the theme "AI-Governance for Local Impact," the event showcased creative solutions aimed at enhancing accessibility, accuracy, and engagement in governance processes. Below are the detailed results and evaluations for each participating team.

Results Summary

Problem Statement: Many patients, especially in rural areas, receive lab reports filled with medical jargon they can't understand, leading to confusion and risk of misdiagnosis.
Solution: Sehat Guftagu provides instant doctor-style explanations of lab reports in mother tongue Urdu language, as well as English, from educated people, so anyone, anywhere can understand without delays or specialist access.

Team Oblivious - Awaz-e-Policy
Problem Statement: Most citizens in Pakistan find it difficult to understand official government policies due to bureaucratic language and English dominant content, lack of awareness, loss of civic disengagement, limited public accountability.
Solution: AI-powered webapp that translates complex policy docs to simple Urdu summaries, converts summaries into natural voice using Uplift API. Works instantly with any doc - can also uploader ask questions. Accessible to all.
